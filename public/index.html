Options Trade Tracker

A free, privacy-focused tool for tracking your options trading positions and calculating P&L.

Launch App

Features

Track Positions

Paste trade confirmations directly from Thinkorswim. Supports spreads, single options, and complex multi-leg strategies.

Calculate P&L

Automatic profit/loss calculations for open and closed positions. View cumulative performance over time.

Google Sheets Sync

Optionally sync your data to Google Sheets for backup and cross-device access. Your data stays in your Google Drive.

Schwab Integration

Connect your Schwab account to automatically fetch real-time position values. Read-only access keeps your account secure.

Tax Tracking

Mark positions as taxable or non-taxable. Track trades across different account types.

100% Private

Your data is stored locally in your browser. No accounts required. We never see your trading data.

How It Works

  1. Create a Service - Organize trades by strategy or advisory service
  2. Add Trades - Paste trade strings from Thinkorswim or enter manually
  3. Track Performance - View P&L, charts, and position details
  4. Sync (Optional) - Enable Google Sheets sync for backup and multi-device access
  5. Schwab (Optional) - Enable integration to Schwab for Mark to Market

Your Privacy Matters

Options Trade Tracker runs entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or have access to your trading data. Optional integrations with Google and Schwab use industry-standard OAuth - we never see your passwords.
